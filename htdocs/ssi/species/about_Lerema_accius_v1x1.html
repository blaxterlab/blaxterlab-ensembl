About Lerema accius

The Hesperidae is a family of butterflies with moth-like features but DNA-based phylogenies suggest that they are more closely related to other butterflies than the Papillionoidae. The clouded skipper Lerema accius was targeted for sequencing due to its value for comparative genomics[1].
