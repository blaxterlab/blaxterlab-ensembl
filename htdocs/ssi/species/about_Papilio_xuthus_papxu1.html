About Papilio xuthus

(From Wikipedia) Papilio xuthus, also known as the Asian swallowtail, Chinese yellow swallowtail or the Xuthus swallowtail, is a yellow-colored. medium to large sized swallowtail butterfly found in northeast Asia, Northern Myanmar, southern China, Korean Peninsula, Japan (from Hokkaid≈ç to Yaeyama Islands), Siberia, and the Hawaiian Islands.
