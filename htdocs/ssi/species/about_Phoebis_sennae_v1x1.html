(From Wikipedia) Phoebis sennae, the cloudless sulphur or cloudless giant sulphur, is a midsized butterfly in the family Pieridae found in the New World. There are several similar species such as the yellow angled-sulphur (Anteos maerula), which has angled wings, or other sulphurs, which are much smaller.
